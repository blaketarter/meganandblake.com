Boy meets girl

In her words...

We have a couple of mutual friends in common so we kept running into each other a few years before we got together. We were both involved with other people the first couple of times we met. Then one time we had a little bonfire at a friend’s house and after Blake and our mutual friend, Alex, drove away, I turned to my friend and said, “I think I have a crush on Blake.” After that, I started making excuses to hang out together like a poker night (which I didn’t really play) or coming by to have lunch with friends that happened to work with Blake almost weekly. I was not subtle. It didn’t take long to realize we had a lot in common and had a lot of fun together.

In his words...

By the time we started hanging out beyond just running into each other, I wasn't really looking for a relationship. But nevertheless she kept coming around and I noticed I was having some of the most fun I'd had in a while. There was this one time we ran into each other at a mutual friends art show, and we just goofed around the entire time. We even put lightning bolt stickers on our foreheads and walked around telling people "We were the boy who lived". Having times like that and enjoying our shared sense of humor made me realize the feelings I had and I never looked back.
